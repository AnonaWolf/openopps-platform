Announcement

OPM’s USAJOBS is proud to announce the launch of Acquisition Open Opportunities. Through a partnership with the Office of Federal Procurement Policy (OFPP), the existing Open Opportunities site will now include a portal for acquisition specific opportunities. Through skill sharing opportunities, acquisition personnel can learn new capabilities through hands-on/experiential training. Just search for opportunities by clicking on the “Acquisition” link under the “Career Field” label to get started today!
